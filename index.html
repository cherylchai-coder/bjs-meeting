Section 01

AI Market Perspective

The evolution from chatbots to autonomous AI — and the gap no one has closed yet.

The Evolution

Three waves of AI in customer experience

2020 – 2023

Chatbots

Rule-based. Scripted decision trees. “Press 1 for billing.”

  • Keyword matching
  • FAQ deflection
  • No memory between sessions
  • High containment, low resolution
2024 – 2025

AI Agents

LLM-powered. Can understand context, converse naturally, resolve issues. But still reactive.

  • Natural language understanding
  • Can resolve, not just deflect
  • Session-based intelligence
  • Waits for the customer to ask
2026+

Autonomous AI

Proactive. Memory-powered. Coordinates across parties. Acts on the member’s behalf.

  • Persistent memory across sessions
  • Reaches out before you ask
  • Contacts external parties autonomously
  • Earns more autonomy through data

Section 03

Agent Steward

From human bottleneck to AI resolution. The capability that turns Bev into a concierge that acts.

The Problem

The double work problem

5 min
Job 1: Decide
Review evidence. Make a judgment call.
vs.
5 hrs
Job 2: Communicate
Everything else that follows the decision.
  • Contact warehouse about the issue
  • Follow up with the member
  • Update internal systems
  • Send confirmation & next steps
  • Coordinate with delivery partner

Steward eliminates Job 2. Humans make decisions. AI owns coordination and communication.

Architecture

How Steward works

Member Reports an issue
→
AI Agent Bev Detects complex issue
→
Capability Steward Activates Spins up sub-agents
↓
Sub-Agent 1 Investigation Checks records, policies, history
Sub-Agent 2 External Outreach Contacts warehouse, vendor, driver
Sub-Agent 3 Member Updates Keeps member informed throughout
↓
Result Summary + Recommendation Delivered to human agent for approval — or executed autonomously once earned

The Path to Autonomy

Every level is earned through data

Level 0
Today: Human Does Everything
Human investigates, decides, communicates. Customer waits. Agent juggles browser tabs.
Level 1
Steward Launch: AI Recommends, Human Approves
Steward investigates across systems, contacts external parties, synthesizes evidence, proposes resolution. Human reviews and approves.
“Steward investigated 3 systems, contacted warehouse, found a shipping error. Recommended: full replacement. Approve / Modify / Reject.”
Level 2
Graduated Autonomy: Steward Goes End-to-End
Earned through recommendation accuracy data from Level 1. When data shows 95%+ match with human judgment, Steward resolves end-to-end. Each use case earns autonomy independently.
“For curbside missing items under $50, Steward resolves autonomously. Complex returns still go through human approval until they earn it.”

Everyone wants Level 2. Steward is how you get there — with data, not a leap of faith. Every transition is earned through measurable accuracy.

The Insight

The middle path is the fast path

Reality

For simple cases (cancel subscription, check order status), AI can already handle them fully. The technology is there.

Hesitancy

But businesses aren’t ready. They don’t want AI touching money yet. Refunds, credits, billing adjustments — the stakes feel too high for day one.

Steward

Steward at L1 proves accuracy across hundreds of cases. Every recommendation is compared against what the human actually decided. When the data shows 95%+ alignment — graduate to L2.

Result

Steward earns its own retirement from human oversight. Not a compromise. Not “you need humans forever.” A speedrun to full autonomy with proof at every step.

What we learned from the market: Enterprise buyers universally want full automation. They told us directly: “We want AI to handle everything autonomously.” That’s exactly where Steward leads — but with a data trail that earns trust along the way.
