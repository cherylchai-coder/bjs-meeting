The Vision for
AI Concierge

BJ’s Wholesale Club × Delight AI

What We Believe

An AI that remembers your members is an AI that drives revenue.

8 million people chose BJ’s over Costco. Over Sam’s Club. They’re not just buying bulk — they’re buying into a brand. An AI that remembers their preferences, knows their Club+ rewards, and catches their expiring coupons isn’t a chatbot. It’s Bev — your brand, in every conversation.

What We Believe

Membership is a relationship.

The member who’s been with you 5 years. The one who shops every Saturday. The one whose kid just went to college and her cart changed completely. Bev should notice. That’s what makes someone renew — not the 2% back, but feeling known.

What We Believe

Your members also need an AI that fights for them.

Today, the industry average is 50% human handoff. We think that’s 50% giving up. The moment a case gets hard — a missing delivery, a billing dispute, a return that needs the warehouse — AI quits and hands it to a human.

What We Believe

Support shouldn’t be the worst part of membership.

Instead, it’s hold music. Transfers. “Let me look into that and get back to you.” They’re paying $60–$120 a year to belong. They deserve better.

AI that remembers + AI that acts

The only two capabilities that matter.

AI in Customer Experience

We’re entering the third wave

Wave 1 · 2020–2023

Chatbots

  • • Rule-based & scripted
  • • Keyword matching
  • • No memory between sessions
  • • High containment, low resolution
Wave 2 · 2024–2025

AI Agents

  • • LLM-powered
  • • Can understand & resolve
  • • Still reactive — waits for the ask
  • • Session-based, no persistence
Wave 3 · 2026+

Autonomous AI

  • • Memory-powered & proactive
  • • Reaches out before you ask
  • • Contacts external parties
  • • Earns autonomy through data
What Wave 3 Requires

What autonomous AI actually requires

Memory

  • Knows purchase history
  • Preferences persist across sessions
  • Builds a living customer profile
